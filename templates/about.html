Instructions

For the web application to work, the user must have a Dexcom CGM (Continuous Glucose Monitor) and their preffered viewing device connected with their Dexcom account. (Ex: smart device connected with CGM and Dexcom throught Dexcom app)

Click the analyze tab and input the days for which you want to view your blood sugars and hit submit.

Log into your Dexcom account and view your selected data!

Note: keep track of your carbohydrate intakes and insulin injections on Dexcom app to view them!
